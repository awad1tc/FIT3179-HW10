New York City Food Week 2018

This webpage contains visulisations examining reviews of restaurants that pariticipated in New York City Food Week 2018. We will examine restaurant review scores over various dimensions by cuisine, price range and other factors. Enjoy!

New York City Restaurants by Food Review and Price Range

The following map visualises the reviews received by restaurants duing New York Food Week 2018. We can see that there is a strong concentration of restaurants in downtown Manhattan, with a few in Staten Island and Brooklyn. Queens and The Bronx did not have any restaurants reviewed across the data.

Manhattan Restaurants by Food Review and Price Range

As the vast majority of restaurants are located in Manhattan, this map of Manhattan only gives a clearer idea of how these restaurants are distributed.

Average Review Scores by Restaurant Type

This visulaisation shows the average reviews scores by category for each restaurant type against the overall average review score given to each restaurant taking into account all factors. It is interesting to see that the majority of restaurants receive category reviews on average that are worse than the overall average

Cheap vs. Expensive Restaurants

More expensive restaurants have higher food reviews, however this is more pronoucned for American and Contemporary American cuisine. French restuarnats have the smallest variance across price range.

Mid Priced vs. Expensive Restaurants

For almost all cuisines review scores are closer for mid priced restaurants. For French and Mediterranean restuarnats the mid priced reviews are worse than the cheap restaurants.

This visualisation is created by Alex Waddington. The datasource is the NYC Food Week 2018 dataset available from Kaggle.
